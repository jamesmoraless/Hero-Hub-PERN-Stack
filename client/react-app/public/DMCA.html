DMCA Notice & Takedown Policy

Introduction

This document explains our policy regarding the handling of DMCA notices and takedown requests.

Copyright Complaints

To file a DMCA complaint, please provide detailed information including the copyrighted work, the infringing material, and your contact details.

Takedown Process

Upon receiving a valid DMCA complaint, the alleged infringing content will be removed promptly.

Counter-Notice

Content providers can file a counter-notice if they believe the content was mistakenly identified as infringing.

Repeat Infringer Policy

Users who repeatedly violate copyright laws may face account suspension or termination.

Contact Information

Send DMCA notices and inquiries to jmorale6@uwo.ca.
